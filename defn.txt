 Output parsers in langchain to convert raw LLM responses Into structured format like JSON,
 CSV, pydantic models and more.They  ensure consistency validation and ease of use in applications


 structuredoutput parsers is an output parser in langchain that helps to extract JSON data 
 from LLM responses based on predefined field schemas.It works by defining a list of fields
 that the model should return ensuring the output follows a structured format
Disadvantage-No  Data validation
